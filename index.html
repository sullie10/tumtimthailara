Thai BBQ

Embark on a sizzling journey with our Thai BBQ, an experience that is just as interactive as it is delicious. Popularly known as 'Moo Kratha', which translates to 'pork skillet', Thai BBQ is a staple of Thai street food culture, offering a delightful blend of grilling and hotpot cooking right at your table.

SPECIAL DISHES

Moo Yang

Thai Moo Yang is authentic barbecued pork. Pig marinated with garlic, coriander root, pepper, palm sugar, and fish sauce smells sweet, flavourful, and fragrant. Seared marinated pork is delicious. Moo Yang with spicy tamarind dipping sauce showcases Thai umami.Thai cuisine lovers may enjoy Moo Yang with sticky rice and papaya salad.
